House Extension

Location: West London

Duration: 3 months

Type: Residential Extension

Project Overview

This modern glass extension transformed a traditional West London home into a bright, contemporary living space. The project included:

  • Design and construction of a 30 sqm glass extension
  • Integration with existing architecture
  • Energy-efficient glazing system
  • Underfloor heating installation
  • Landscaping of the surrounding garden area

Key Features

Space Optimization

Maximized living space while maintaining garden area

Natural Light

Floor-to-ceiling glass walls for maximum daylight

Eco-Friendly

Energy-efficient materials and systems
